Joe ensured Santa that he would carry out his mission to touch the hearts of many, and to give the gift of Belief if only for a moment.

Joe and Ted feel very strongly about giving back to the community. "We have been very blessed in this life" because of this we want to show the love to those who may not receive a gift at Christmas. As children we have all believed that Santa exists, and look forward for his return throughout the year. Think of the many young and old who have lost the joy of believing.

The Santa Letter Project wants to give this back during a special time of year. so please help us spread the love and BELIEVE.
