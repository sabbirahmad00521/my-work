FUND RASING MADE EASY
THE SANTA LETTER PROJECT WAY

Make the Call and let The Santa Letter Project help you raise the profits you need to fund your needs. 40% to your organization for every package sold

  • Call or E -Mail The Santa Letter Project to register your group and to set up your fund raising dates
  • A Santa Letter Project consultant will help you get started, and will work with you from the beginning the end of the event to ensure you have a successful campaign
  • All the needed materials will be sent or delivered to you to begin your fund raising
  • NO MONEY OR PURCHASE OF PRODUCT WILL BE NEEDED TO BEGING YOUR CAMPAIGN
  • No minimum order or amount of sales required. You determine what you want to raise
  • At the completion of the campaign you keep 40% of the proceeds and send the remainder to The Santa Letter Project.
    • Your Tasks:
  • Involve the entire community
  • Begin posting on social media, send e-mail, make announcements, post flyers, mailers, set up a table at an event.../li>
  • Plan an event
  • Say thank you
The best way to ensure your fundraiser runs smoothly Is to plan ahead.
  • Meet with your committee to plan your course of action. Have a copy of the fundraising kit for each member.
  • Review the successes and setbacks you experienced with previous fund raisers to determine what could be done differently.
  • Set a fundraising goal
  • Meet with your volunteers to distribute tasks and set deadlines.
  • Prepare materials for your group members like notices or flyers that give them all the information they will need to participate.
  • Fundraising doesn't have to be stressful. If you take the time to plan and get organized before you start raising funds, your campaign will be a Successl
