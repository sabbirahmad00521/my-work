Complete Investment Solution

By providing a complete investment solution for families, institutions, and endowments/foundations, Woodmont Investment Counsel helps our clients achieve their financial goals. In the rapidly changing investment advisory industry, it is the vision of Woodmont Investment Counsel to be a leader in innovative ideas and performance, blending cutting edge technology with sophisticated investment advice.

OUR CLIENTS

Individuals

Our typical client has a net worth of at least $2 million, with at least $1 million of investable assets.

Families

From our close work with family enterprises, we understand families are unique. We partner with families in a multi-generational context and in collaboration with other advisors to meet specific goals.

Business Owners

Woodmont works closely with business owners in several ways to manage their liquid assets, as well as lay out a comprehensive investment plan in conjunction with any sale or transaction of the business. As well, we provide 401(k) plan administration to business owners and their employees.

Institutions

We work with institutions to provide investment advisory services, as well as 401(k) plan administration. We have substantial experience in working with investment committees to help organizations articulate and achieve their investment priorities.
