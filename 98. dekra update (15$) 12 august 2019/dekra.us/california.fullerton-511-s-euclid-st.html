What is a STAR Smog Check Station?

STAR Stations are Smog Check stations in the State of California that meet higher performance standards established by the Bureau of Automotive Repair.

DEKRA stations are STAR Certified "Test Only" stations, therefore, no repairs are performed.

What do I need to bring to my Smog Check?

Bring your current proof of Vehicle Registration or Registration Renewal Notice, and a form of payment.
