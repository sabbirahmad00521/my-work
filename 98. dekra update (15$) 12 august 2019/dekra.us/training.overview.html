ISO Training

Are you looking for cost-effective ISO training to help your staff and organization? We have a variety of solutions for you.

In 2016, DEKRA acquired AQS Solutions, who has been providing training since 1989. We offer training in support of organizational improvement and implementation of international management system standards. We successfully participated in the RAB pilot project for ISO 9001 Lead Auditor Training in 1993 and the ISO 14001 Lead Auditor Training in 1996.

We offer training publicly, as well as on-site at your office or satellite location.
