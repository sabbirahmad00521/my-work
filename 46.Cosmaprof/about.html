ABOUT US

Cosmaprof Co.,Ltd.

Cosmaprof is a professional cosmetics manufacturer located on Ram Kam Haeng road, Bangkok, with an operating space of more than 30,000 square feet.

Being the leader in the cosmetic industry we are committed to providing high-end production technology, professional employees including scientists, pharmacologists, and chemists who are responsible for product research and production. In order to retain first place as a cosmetics manufacturer and fulfil our diverse customer base which include dermatologists, cosmetic professionals, and brand owners. Cosmaprof has fine-tuned its process to meet different budget segments while still being able to advise customers on every level regarding formulation, packaging, labelling, printing and advertising. Our work process will make sure that customers are well-served and their business can perform effectively.

Management

Cosmaprof was founded in April 2004 by Dr. Pawin Visatepattaya, a specialist in skin cosmetics. In 2008 a Canadian-Dutch shareholder joined the company which increased the companies’ customer base internationally. Today we send out products to more than 25 countries all over the world.

Cosmaprof values the importance of having well-trained and excellent personnel so we make it a priority to train our staff and bring out their full potential. We make sure that our workers have a healthy life-style and feel like they are part of a big family.

Production Capacity

Cosmaprof uses about 1,000 kilograms of raw material for production per day, yielding approximately 20,000 pieces of merchandise (this includes manufacturing and labelling). Our standard products are categorised in two big group, by product segment (such as lightening products, sunscreen, etc.) or by customer skin type. Our management team is comprised of experienced workers from different fields such as chemical engineering, marketing, and business management. This ensures that your relationship with us will be an easy and satisfactory one. We also have a great sales force team who are proactive about servicing our clients. So, it can be concluded that our wide range of staff will make our relationship a smooth one and you will never miss a chance to generate income.
