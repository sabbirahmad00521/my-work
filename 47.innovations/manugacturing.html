Quality Control

Our ISO international standardized and FDA Certified factories utilize the latest in cutting edge technology and state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment to produce the highest quality products under strict quality standards, these standards are met from all aspects of products from raw materials in formulization right down to the dispatch of goods.

Production Line

While in production, products are inspected at multiple intervals on the production line, we endeavor to ensure that every product which leaves the factory is of the highest standard, quality and to perfection. All products are mixed, filled, capped, labeled, coded and wrapped under scrupulous quality control procedures and standards on all of our production lines.

Shipping & Logistic Services

OEM Innovations can take the stress out of all your shipping requirements and handle with ease all aspects of point to point logistics, we ensure compliance and required documentation for both internationally and domestic deliveries is in line with each and every order for ease of clearance at the end destination.

OEM Innovations currently exports cosmetic products to customers throughout China, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Japan, Taiwan and Hong Kong with customer expansion into the Middle Eastern, European, Russian and South America and Australasian markets coming soon. We are here to ensure your goods are shipped in a timely manner with ease and efficiency.
