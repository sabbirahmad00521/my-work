Packaging

OEM Innovation manufactures and supply extensive ranges of premium quality packaging options from PET Bottles, Airless Containers, Droppers, Jars and Flexi Tubes in various sizes, and shapes.

Minimum MOQ of 5000 units per order, order turn around for all packaging direct from Korea is 2-4 approximately weeks dependent on packaging type, order volume and specific availability, alternate package sourcing is available from other global vendors if required, in addition we can design, manufacture and print outer carton packaging to specific requirements.

You can never underestimate the importance and persuasive value of appearance. Our packaging options can give you that aesthetic edge to further compliment the product within.

Graphic Design

Our skilled team of in house designers are here to assist customers in the creation of all areas of graphic design and imagery. As we are experts in the industry, and specialize in design for skin and body care products for the ever changing trends of the cosmetic industry we possess the knowledge and expertise in all aspects of private label artwork to bring a modern appealing look to your selected product or range. This is just one of the advantages that our customers have when working with OEM Innovations in creating a new brand or adding to an existing product line.

Our designers work closely with our customers to calibrate ideas and proof all designs.

All individual jobs are 100 percent customized to ensure originality.

Endless options are available for packaging pint from Silkscreen, Offset & Hot Stamping to create an ascetically remarkable logo and or design to your product ready for sale in the consumer market.

All product information from instructional use to product benefits and purposes is drafted and sent to each and every customer for review and then applied and printed to each and every unit of product produced. Product information can be applied in any language; our international translators are on hand to accommodate all international customers requirements.
