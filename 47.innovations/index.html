Company Overview

OEM Innovations Co Ltd was established in 2009, and are forefront leaders in OEM/ODM/R&D Manufacturing,Trading, Distribution and Wholesaling of functional Korean made cosmetics to the ASEAN and international markets.

OEM Innovation applies world leading technology to our comprehensive range of cosmetics using new BIO & natural materials, with advanced expertise in all areas of dermatology technology and sciences to bring innovative and cutting edge products to our customers for the global consumer purchasing market.

Our expert in house team delivers to the highest of standards to each and every customer from initial stages of development to specialty packaging creations as well as all areas of graphic design and print, right down to point to point logistics to ensure your product is received and launched successfully to your expectations and vision within a short turnaround.
