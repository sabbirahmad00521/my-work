About Moro

Moro is a UAE-based digital data hub focused on both transformation and operational execution services that support our clients’ digital agenda.

We offer data, integration and managed services supported by a world-class alliance network, ensuring agility and cost competitiveness within an exemplar digital framework. Our unique, fully certified facility includes a state of the art data center and provides full business continuity services.

At Moro we believe that the world would be a better place if trust was the shared currency in business, allowing each partner to focus on what they do best. That’s why we place trust and clear commitments at the heart of everything we do.

Service

colocation

Moro provides colocation and managed hosting services to customers’ critical IT infrastructure. The purpose-built data center with its government grade security is a UAE carrier neutral facility catered to by a rich ecosystem of clients, partners and alliance members.

cloud services

Moro’s Enterprise Cloud platform is designed specifically for handling complex IT landscapes with a specialty in mission-critical applications; it runs complex business applications with better performance, greater agility, higher security and superior economics.

Moro’s Enterprise cloud supports:

  • • Flexible and hybrid deployments
  • • Virtual private or public architecture
  • • Government architecture zones
  • • Utility consumption based pricing

Moro’s Enterprise Cloud provides a management portal with:

  • • Greater control and automation
  • • Usage and SLA Reporting
managed services

From infrastructure to application, Moro offers a broad range of managed services including design, planning, migration, monitoring and infrastructure optimization, supporting clients into their digital transformation journey.

professional services

Our team provides comprehensive integration solution implementation, handling all facets of project delivery on B2B or enterprise application integration initiatives. This also covers Master Data Management (MDM) implementation projects, including customer and product data initiatives.

business services

Moro delivers catered business solutions, including call center services, business continuity services and serviced offices, allowing clients to properly function from its data hub.
