1 BUILD YOUR FRESH ORDER

Once you are registered it’s time to begin having real food from our farms and producers delivered right to your front door! Choose from over 200 products: dairy, meats, produce, bakery items, and pantry products—whatever you select you can be sure that the products are natural and sourced from producers that share our passion for our food-system.

2 Choose how often

Know you want a regular, recurring order? You can set up weekly, every other week, or monthly. Get some products weekly and others biweekly. Or, order as you go. Frequency is entirely up to you! No contracts, no hassle. Simply what you want, when you want it.

3We Deliver

All you need to do is leave an insulated cooler (or milk box) outside the night before your delivery. Our milkmen will do the rest! On hot summer nights we recommend you leave frozen ice packs in the cooler, and on winter nights where temps fall below freezing, we recommend leaving towels to help keep the milk from freezing.

4 YOU ENJOY!

Wake up to find farm-fresh goodness at your door! Now it’s time for you and your family to enjoy local, non-processed, farm-fresh products. You will taste and LOVE the difference!
