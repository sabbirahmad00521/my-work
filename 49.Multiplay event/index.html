OUR EVENTS

Insomnia Gaming Festival

Multiplay Events are the organizers of the UK’s biggest gaming festival, an event which is designed and developed by our internal team, to bring all aspects of gaming under one roof at it’s home of the NEC, Birmingham UK.

BRICKLIVE

Multiplay Events are the organizers of the UK’s biggest celebration of LEGO®, spread across different venues around the UK. Our events team create a world class event, with every aspect created in-house, from conception to execution.

Blockfest

From event management to logistics. Crafted in collaboration with Creators and Talent alike, Blockfest is a Minecraft event built with the community, for the community.

iMusic

Working with Universal Music, Multiplay Events was able to bring 5 international artists to the Insomnia Gaming Festival. From the stage design to the event delivery, Multiplay Events was able to deliver a world class music event.

Minecon

Co-producers of MINECON Paris, Orlando, London, LA and MINECON Earth London & Atlanta.
MINECON is an annual celebration of all things Minecraft.

Runefest

Runefest is an annual community event which celebrates Jagex Studios’ flagship game series, RuneScape. Multiplay Events act as principal supplier for this series, not only delivering a huge gaming LAN for guests to enjoy but also, a fully-themed, world-class studio stream and an award nominated main stage showcasing many different panel talks, update reveals, and a whole host of evening entertainment.

Call of Duty World League 2017

On behalf of Activision, we delivered the largest European only leg of the Call of Duty World League circuit. With a prize pool of $50,000, 64 teams battled it out to be number one, taking home the CWL trophy and the all important circuit points.

Hearthstone Truesilver Championship I, II & III

Multiplay Events very own major esports tournament, which was watched by 2,500,000 people across the world. Working with Blizzard guidance, Multiplay Events was able to deliver all 3 events from the construction of IP & Design to the running of each tournament.
